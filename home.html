About Me

Hey, I'm Tamilarasan D, just a regular 20-year-old from Salem, Tamil Nadu. Whether I'm exploring virtual worlds or building my own, I'm always up for the challenge. Learning is my jam – whether it's mastering a new programming language or diving into the latest gaming trends, I'm all in.
